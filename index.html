How This Age Calculator Works

This tool calculates your exact age from your date of birth to today's date. It provides your age in years, months and days, along with total days lived and time remaining until your next birthday.

How To Calculate Age Manually

To calculate age manually, subtract the birth year from the current year, then adjust based on whether your birthday has occurred yet this year.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate my exact age?

Use your date of birth and subtract it from today's date.

Does this include leap years?

Yes. The calculator uses actual calendar date differences.
